This is one of the simplest microwave repairs we handle. The turntable connects to the motor through a small plastic drive coupler underneath the tray, and that coupler is designed to wear out before the motor does — it's an inexpensive part that we can usually replace in minutes.

If the coupler and roller guide both look fine, the turntable motor itself may have failed, which is a slightly bigger repair but still straightforward. We check the simple, cheap parts first since they're the more common cause by far.
